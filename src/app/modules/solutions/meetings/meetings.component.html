Video conferencing bridges and connectors

Video conferencing bridges connect different systems together in one meeting. Participants are connected in a single virtual meeting room, regardless of the system they use or the location from which they join.

Bridges and connectors enable all video meeting participants to join the meeting, see and hear everyone, and share content. Proscene works with well-known video conference bridge service providers including Poly, and Pexip. We help ensure all participants can join the meeting and collaborate, whether they join from a room system or via a cloud application.
